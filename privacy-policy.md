# Privacy Policy for PosturePal

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

## Introduction

PosturePal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Data
- PosturePal uses your device's camera to analyze your posture in real-time
- **All camera processing happens entirely on your device**
- We do NOT transmit, store, or upload any camera images or video to external servers
- No facial recognition or biometric data is collected or stored

### Session Data
- Posture session statistics (duration, posture scores, alert counts)
- This data is stored locally on your device only
- Session data is never uploaded to our servers

### Purchase Information
- If you subscribe to PosturePal Pro, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play
- We receive confirmation of your subscription status from RevenueCat (our payment processor)
- We do NOT have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.)

### Analytics Data
- We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics
- This includes app opens, feature usage, and crash reports
- This data cannot be used to identify you personally

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide posture monitoring functionality
- To track your posture improvement over time (stored locally)
- To process and manage your subscription
- To improve our app and fix bugs

## Data Storage

- All posture data is stored locally on your device
- We do not maintain servers that store your personal data
- Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data

## Third-Party Services

### RevenueCat
- Manages subscription purchases
- Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Google Play Services
- Handles payment processing
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

PosturePal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (by clearing app data or uninstalling)
- Opt out of analytics (in app settings, if available)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
